InnovateINW

Helping organizations innovate and grow.

InnovateINW exists to help founders, researchers, and business leaders innovate, grow, and succeed while strengthening the Inland Northwest’s innovation economy.

Get Involved Follow us on LinkedIn

Our Purpose

No one should have to build—or grow—a company alone.

InnovateINW is a regional innovation initiative and a guide for founders, researchers, and business leaders to solve problems and accelerate growth.

Guide

We connect people with experienced advisors, mentors, industry experts, and peer networks.

Connect

We bring together technology, university research and talent, capital, and regional partnerships.

Grow

We support commercialization, business development, market expansion, and sustainable growth.
