Springboard

We help startups validate their idea and get traction.

Springboard is a 90-day, hands-on sprint for Inland Northwest founders who need structure, customer discovery, business model clarity, and a practical path toward momentum.

Apply to Springboard See the Process
What it is

A founder sprint built around validation.

Springboard helps early-stage tech and life science companies in the Inland Northwest test the business, learn from customers, and prepare for the next right step.

The goal is not to make founders look busy. The goal is to help companies gain traction and become more investable through a clear process, expert feedback, and community support.

Springboard is for founders who need:

  • Problem and customer validation
  • Lean canvas and business model development
  • Go-to-market thinking and early traction
  • Pitch, advisor, and investor readiness
Process

From idea to traction.

The process moves founders through foundation building, customer discovery, and business development — building confidence and clarity.

Phase 1

Foundation Building

  1. Initial meeting and approval
  2. Lean canvas development
  3. Customer discovery
  4. Problem / solution fit
Phase 2

Build, Measure, Learn

  1. MVP or early product learning
  2. Market and customer testing
  3. Go-to-market strategy
  4. Traction milestones
Phase 3

Business Development

  1. Financial, legal, and operations review
  2. Pitch deck development
  3. Advisor pitch sessions
  4. Program graduation
Learning Loop

Build. Measure. Learn.

Springboard keeps founders focused on the loop that matters: build the smallest useful experiment, measure what customers actually do, and learn what to change next.

That rhythm keeps the work practical. Founders leave with evidence, not just opinions.
